Analyzes continuous and discrete-time signals and systems, covering Linear Time-Invariant (LTI) modeling, Laplace transforms, convolution integrals, Fourier analysis, transfer functions, and the fundamentals of signal sampling. The course emphasizes the mathematical foundations of frequency-domain analysis and the practical application of MATLAB/Simulink for system verification.

## üõ† Technical Competencies

Demonstrated engineering principles through the coursework include:

* **System Analysis:** Characterization of Linear Time-Invariant (LTI) systems using Impulse and Step responses.
* **Transform Methods:** Application of Laplace Transforms and Fourier Analysis (Series & Transform) for frequency-domain solving.
* **Signal Processing:** Implementation of convolution integrals and analysis of signal sampling/reconstruction.
* **Simulation & Modeling:** Utilization of **MATLAB** and **Simulink** to visualize signal properties and verify theoretical models.
* **Filter Design:** Identification of system characteristics required for signal shaping and filtering.
